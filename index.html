How Much Is Your Dollar Worth?

A Global Inflation Dashboard

This site is a proposal and proof of concept for the competition hosted by 1729 and sponsored by chainlink.

The goal of this dashboard is to be able to track global inflation in a way that is not censored by the governments. This way investors and economists can view the increase of price in goods from an unbiased source.
